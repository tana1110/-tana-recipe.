Icd Coffee Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Instant Coffee

  • Sugar

  • Milk

  • Ice

  • Cup

  • Popsicle

How to Prepare

  1. Put the coffee and suger in another cup, pour some warm water over them, then mix them well.

  2. In the main cup, pour the milk and add the amount of ice that suits you.

  3. Finally pour the cofee mixture over the milk and ice and enjoy.

    4. You can add any topping you like; Caramel sauce, chocolate sauce, erc..
